3.1.1 z distribution

From the population data, we want to estimate the mean of the height using sampling, with n=30.

height_30<-sample(classData$身高,30,replace = T)
height_30

From this sampling result, we can have point estimation of the mean

mean(height_30)
## [1] 167.25

Because n>30, the distribution of sample mean is normal distribution. We can estimate 95% confidence interval using z distribution

se<-sd.p(classData$身高)/sqrt(30)
mean(height_30)-1.96*se
## [1] 164.079
mean(height_30)+1.96*se
